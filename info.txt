Welcome to FermFarm!

In FermFarm you grow crops, ferment them, and sell your produce for coins. Here is everything you need to know to get started.

BUYING SEEDS

Click one of the seed bags in the shop on the right side of the screen to buy a seed. Each seed costs a small amount of coins. After clicking a bag your cursor is loaded with that seed type.

PLANTING

With a seed selected, click any empty plot in the soil grid at the bottom of the screen to plant it. The seed will begin growing automatically as days pass.

HARVESTING

When a crop has fully grown right-click it in the grid to harvest it. Harvested crops are added to your inventory and you earn coins automatically.

FERMENTATION

You can ferment harvested crops for bonus coins using the two pots in the shop area. The small pot ferments one crop at a time. The large pot ferments two crops at once. Fermented crops are worth more than selling raw but you earn coins immediately when you place them in the pot.

THE CALENDAR

The circle on the calendar in the top right tracks how many days have passed. Every few seconds a new day begins and your crops grow one stage closer to harvest.

SAVING AND LOADING

Press ESC to open the pause menu. Right-click any save slot to save your current farm to that slot. Left-click a filled slot to load it and resume from where you left off.

FULLSCREEN AND WINDOWED

Use the Windowed and Fullscreen buttons in the pause menu to switch display modes. You can also press F11 at any time.

TIPS

Good luck on your farm!
