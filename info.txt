Welcome to FermFarm!

In FermFarm you grow crops, ferment them, and sell your produce for coins. Here is everything you need to know to get started.


BUYING SEEDS

Click one of the seed bags on the shop shelves on the right side of the screen to buy a seed. Hover over a bag to see its price. After clicking, your cursor is loaded with that seed type. Right-click or press ESC to cancel and get your coins back.


PLANTING

With a seed selected, left-click any empty owned tile in the soil grid at the bottom of the screen to plant it. Seeds will not grow unless you water them each day. You can only plant on tiles you own.


WATERING

Click the water source near the shed to pick up the watering can. Then left-click a planted tile to water it. The normal watering can empties after a single use and must be refilled each time. A water droplet icon appears on tiles that have been watered today.

On rainy days all your crops are watered automatically.


HARVESTING

When a crop has fully grown, right-click it in the grid to harvest it. The harvested crop will follow your cursor. Be careful: once ripe, a plant will die if left unharvested for 2 in-game days. Hover over a plant to see a tooltip showing how many waterings it still needs, or how many days you have left to harvest it.


DEAD PLANTS

If you leave a ripe plant too long it will die and cannot be sold normally. Right-click a dead plant to clear it from the grid. You will receive a small refund equal to the seed price minus 2 coins. Dead plants cannot be watered or grown further.


SELLING

With a harvested crop following your cursor, left-click the chest in the shop to sell it for coins.


CROP PRICES

Seed prices and sell values (raw / fermented):
  Tomato   - seed: 3   raw: 5    fermented: 18
  Carrot   - seed: 6   raw: 10   fermented: 28
  Cucumber - seed: 13  raw: 20   fermented: 45
  Chili    - seed: 18  raw: 20   fermented: 38
  Cabbage  - seed: 23  raw: 26   fermented: 60 #50
  Garlic   - seed: 28  raw: 42 #35   fermented: 90 #60


FERMENTATION

Fermentation gives you much higher sell values than selling raw. To ferment a crop:

1. Buy a fermentation pot from the shop shelf (costs 30 coins). It will follow your cursor.
2. Left-click an empty slot in the shed to place the pot there.
3. With a harvested crop on your cursor, left-click a pot in the shed to place the fruit inside.
4. Wait for the fermentation to finish. A sparkle appears on the pot when it is done.
5. Left-click the finished pot to pick up the fermented crop.
6. Left-click the chest to sell it for the higher fermented value.

Fermentation times by crop:
  Tomato: 2 days, Chili: 2 days, Carrot: 3 days, Cucumber: 3 days, Cabbage: 4 days, Garlic: 5 days

You can place up to 2 pots in the shed at the same time.


PURCHASING TILES

You start with 4 owned tiles in the top-right of the grid. To expand, left-click any locked tile showing a "for sale" sign. Tiles in columns closer to the left are more expensive. Hover over a locked tile to see its price. Prices per column, from right to left: 3, 5, 10, 15, 20, 30, 50 coins.


THE CALENDAR

The circle on the calendar in the top right tracks how many days have passed. Every 5 real-time seconds a new in-game day begins. Crops only grow on days they were watered.


RAIN

On rainy days all planted crops are automatically watered. Rain occurs 2 out of every 12 days and the schedule is randomised each cycle. Check the weather report on screen to know if rain is coming.


GOLDEN WATER BUCKET

Once you have enough coins you can buy the Golden Water Bucket from the shop for 300 coins. Unlike the normal watering can which empties after every single use, the Golden Bucket holds enough water for 10 uses before it needs to be refilled. After buying it once you own it permanently. Hover over it in your hand to see how many uses remain.


SAVING AND LOADING

Press ESC to open the pause menu. Right-click any save slot to save your current farm to that slot. Left-click a filled slot to load it and resume from where you left off. Left-click an empty slot to start a brand new game.


FULLSCREEN AND WINDOWED

Use the Windowed or Fullscreen button in the pause menu to switch display modes. You can also press F11 at any time.


MUSIC

Use the Music button in the pause menu to toggle the background music on or off.


CONTROLS SUMMARY

  Left-click     Plant a seed, water a crop, buy an item, sell fruit, pick up pot
  Right-click    Harvest a ripe or dead crop, cancel a held seed or pot
  ESC            Cancel held item, or open the pause menu
  F11            Toggle fullscreen


TIPS

Check the weather forecast before you water: on rainy days you can skip watering entirely.
Fermented crops are always worth more. The longer a crop ferments the bigger the bonus.
Keep a close eye on fully grown crops. You only have 2 days to harvest before they die.
Buying tiles in the middle columns unlocks more growing space at a moderate cost.
The Golden Bucket is a big upfront investment but saves a lot of time on large farms.

Good luck on your farm!
