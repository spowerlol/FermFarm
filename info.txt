Welcome to FermFarm!

In FermFarm you grow crops, ferment them, and sell your produce for coins. Here is everything you need to know to get started.

BUYING SEEDS

Click one of the seed bags in the shop on the right side of the screen to buy a seed. Each seed costs a small amount of coins. After clicking a bag your cursor is loaded with that seed type.

PLANTING

With a seed selected, click any empty plot in the soil grid at the bottom of the screen to plant it. The seed will begin growing as days pass, but you do need to water them each day.

HARVESTING

When a crop has fully grown right-click it in the grid to harvest it. Harvested crops are added to your inventory, and you earn coins by left-clicking on the chest.
If you are too late with harvesting the plant will die, and you will get automatically a penalty of 2 coins from the seed price when you harvest the plant

PURCHASING TILES

To expand your farming empire you can purchase more tiles. This can be done by clicking with your left mouse button on the purchasable tiles with prices ranging per column.
going right to left: 3,5,10,15,20,30,50

FERMENTATION

You can ferment harvested crops for bonus coins by buying a pot in the shop and placing the pot in the shed. after that you can place a harvested fruit in the pot.
After the pot is filled you have to wait for 2 days and then with left mouse click you can click on the pot and the fermented fruit will follow the mouse.
Then with left-click on the chest in the shop you can sell the fermented crop.

THE CALENDAR

The circle on the calendar in the top right tracks how many days have passed. Every few seconds a new day begins and your crops grow one stage closer to harvest, if you have watered the crop.
The day counter will keep going but the fruit stages will grow if watered.

SAVING AND LOADING

Press ESC to open the pause menu. Right-click any save slot to save your current farm to that slot. Left-click a filled slot to load it and resume from where you left off.

FULLSCREEN AND WINDOWED

Use the Windowed and Fullscreen buttons in the pause menu to switch display modes. You can also press F11 at any time.

GOLDEN WATERBUCKET
If you have achieved 500 coins, you can buy a GOLDEN waterbucket in the shop, with this bucket you can water the plant for 10 uses before refill instead of 1 use per refill.


TIPS
Look at the weather forecast, then you will know if you need to water your crops.
Keep an eye on your crops, when they are dead there is no turning back.
Try to use the fermentation as much as possible, the bonus is very rewarding and is only takes 2 DAYS.

Good luck on your farm!
